David
The group work for assignment 3 felt a little harder to me than it did for assignment 2. I think that in the end things finally started coming together but it did feel like a challenge to get all the pieces of this project together. The communication of the group could definitely be improved. Not all team members were as active in the group as others which did present some challenges in organising for tasks to be completed. This also meant that the workload was not always distributed evenly as some team members took on additional tasks that had not yet been completed. Our team meetings were generally productive, with those present putting ideas forward, reviewing current progress and setting goals for the following week. Despite the challenges that come with group work, I was surprised at the quality of work we have been able to achieve. I think that we have a solid idea and have made some good progress on it since assignment 2. One thing I have learned from group work is that it is important to make sure that there is a strong lead, and everyone is given equal chance to contribute. I think we did this well by discussing it at the very beginning of our group work and laying out a schedule and structure for the rest of the project.

Hermanto

One thing that I have learned about groups is though we are a diverse group of people, almost all of us are driven to a common goal.

Leigh
This time around the group work was definitely more challenging. Whilst we had the idea of what we wanted to do, I think a lot of time was spent on producing artefacts instead of developing the content and structure for the body of work. It was also just difficult as well as there were unforeseen circumstances that impacted some users. From the group meetings it felt like we were on the right path, but coming up to the deadline it became apparent that there were further obstacles we had to overcome. However some group members were able to pick up extra work and really played as the perfect group members, putting their hand up to help out wherever possible without being prompted. Having such a diverse group as well really created a space where ideas could be challenged and worked at from different angles until we could come to a conclusion. It was rare that an idea was perfect from the start, but through the group process we could shape it to be. As challenging as it was, it was still a great experience to work with some talented people.
