Team Profile - Insignia

Group Process and Communication

In Assignment 2 the group worked pretty well, with most people regularly attending the group meetings held on Microsoft Teams as well as contributing weekly within the discussion boards on Microsoft Teams as well. For this assignment we have introduced some new processes to help the flow of the assignment but also to assist in the reporting of it. Instead of having giant files all over the place, each section was it's own dedicated file that allowed for easy accessibility and editing. We also implemented a 'running sheet' for our reference list. Instead of having references out of order and formatted incorrectly, after doing an intext reference a group member would add the refence to the running list where they could then ask for help or see how others had formatted their references.
