Hermanto Surjani's Career Plan

CTO / Chief Technology Officer

My career goal is to become a CTO or Chief Technology / Technical Officer.
Key to my decision is that I want to be the key decision maker for all the strategic IT plans for a corporation.
Although generally a CTO does not require in depth skills in IT, one should be able to understand and encompass the opportunities and constraints of each technology type, to make strategic decisions that benefit the corporation and the wider community.

    Some of the main responsibilities include:
  • Managing the IT strategy
  • Ensuring IT security and privacy
  • Ensuring IT resiliency
  • Making key decisions on the management of the IT
  • Searching for ways to improve the IT architecture and solutions
  • Managing direct reports in the IT department
  • Being the person responsible or the IT department
Short Term (0 - 5 Years) Mid Term (5 - 10 Years) Long Term (10+ Years)
  • Complete Bachelor of Information Technology course
  • Complete further industry certifications ie. PMP, Scrum master, ITIL
  • Continue working hard in career, possibly changing companies to achieve promotion from current role (Project Manager)
  • Achieve Bachelor of Information Technology degree
  • Achieve IT industry certifications
  • Enroll for MBA course
  • Become a Team Manager or IT Department Manager via current employer promotion or changing companies
  • Complete MBA
  • Achieve multiple IT certifications, managerial certifications.
  • Gain experience as a manager
  • Become a CTO or CIO
