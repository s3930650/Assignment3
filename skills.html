Skills and Jobs

With such a complex operation, outside of our own expertise the Insignia collection will require a wide range of skills to be able to complete hardware, software, and business integrations. Below you'll find some of the job/skills we believe we will require to continue development and accomplish our goals.

Azure Cloud Architect

Full time Role
$160K - $180K + Superannuation

We are looking for an experienced Cloud architect to join the Insignia family. This role will be reporting to our Director of IT and will work alongside our developers, administrators, and other roles to ensure the successful development and implementation of our cloud service requirements. The successful candidate will have the following:

  • Advanced experience and knowledge of IT operations
  • Experience in solution development
  • Ability to determine business requirements, scope, design, and implementation
The following certifications are desirable:
  • Microsoft Azure Solutions Architect Expert
  • Microsoft Certified Azure Security Engineer

Business Intelligence Developer

Full Time Role
$120K - $150K +Superannuation

We are a vibrant start up looking for a skilled and passionate BI developer looking to take their next step. We offer a truly unique role, offering a greenfield opportunity, to establish your own designs and processes. If you are able to provide keen insights and valuable strategic data to our clients we are looking for you. We are looking for an experienced Business Intelligence expert with:

  • Fluent English communication skills both oral and written
  • Ability to integrate with likeminded passionate people
  • The drive to work independently and seek out new challenges
  • Manage customer feedback to provide new and better solutions
  • Leadership experience and team leading or management of project group.
  • Innovative and insightful solutions
The successful applicant will have:
  • 5 years experience in BI development
  • Proven products in the field
  • Knowledge of .NET framework / Microsoft stack
  • Experience with integration of BI solutions in web and desktop
The following certifications are desirable:
  • Microsoft Power Platform Solution Architect
  • Microsoft Certified: Power Platform Solution Architect Expert

Full Stack Developer

Full Time Role
$110K - $130K + Superannuation

Reporting directly to the Lead Developer, the successful applicant will be required to show a strong understanding and comprehension of full stack development. The role requires the design, building, and maintaining of frontend applications for both web and desktop applications. A strong understanding of XAML is required for the desktop applications and Vuejs and TailwindCSS is required for frontend applications. The role also requires further development of the backend of the systems, which is primarily developed in C#.
Key accountabilities:

  • Developing and maintaining UI for both web and desktop development.
  • Developing and maintaining backend solutions.
  • Contributing to planning and development schedules.
  • Following the natural coding standards for each language used.
The successful applicant will have:
  • Strong and professional experience with modern JavaScript frameworks and CSS frameworks.
  • A proven history of desktop development within the .NET Framework.
  • An understanding of the Agile software development life cycle.

Marketing Manager

Full Time Role
$85.5K - $92K + Superannuation

This is a new role created through business growth and an evolution in our brand and marketing strategy. We have exciting projects planned and you will be responsible for the execution of the new marketing strategy and its ongoing evolution liaising with the Brand Manager, Sales Manager, Production Manager, and the Customer Service Team. You will report directly to the operations manager.
Key responsibilities:

  • Working in collaboration with the Brand Manager to enhance and promote our Company name and products and services
  • Competitor analysis
  • Acquisition and retention of customers through optimising current strategies and developing innovative new ones
  • Identifying PR and Partnership opportunities, and working with them to help sell our product
The successful applicant will have the following attributes:
  • Motivated by purpose - you want to make a difference in people's lives
  • Growth obsessed - you are excited by developing and executing innovative strategies to win market share
  • Never complacent - you are prepared to challenge the status quo and embrace new marketing ideas
  • Passionate about customer service and experience - you love to delight customers in ways that drive conversions and revenue
